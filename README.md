## 🧭 About me

I'm a full-stack engineer with **8+ years of hands-on experience** building web products, internal platforms, developer tools, and automation-heavy systems.

I enjoy moving across the whole stack: shaping product-facing interfaces with **React** and **Vue**, designing practical services with **Python** and **Node.js**, and keeping data flows reliable with **SQL**. My favorite kind of work is where product thinking, engineering discipline, and a little bit of automation meet.

- 🧩 I build clean, maintainable frontends with component-driven architecture.
- ⚙️ I design APIs, scripts, and backend services that are simple to operate.
- 🗄️ I work comfortably with relational data models, queries, and business reporting needs.
- 🚀 I care about developer experience, performance, automation, and shipping useful things.
- 🎮 Offline, you'll probably find me in **Dota** or somewhere inside **The Legend of Zelda**.
